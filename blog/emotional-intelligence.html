What is EQ?

Emotional intelligence (otherwise known as emotional quotient or EQ) is the ability to understand, use,

overcome challenges and defuse conflict.



What is IQ?

IQ, or intelligence quotient, is a measure of your ability to reason and solve problems.
It essentially reflects how well you did on a specific test as compared to other people of your age group.



what is the difference between EQ and IQ?

IQ is an inborn ability and general Intelligence.
EQ is learned and improved ability and Emotional Intelligence.



Why EQ is important

The world is to live with other people. That's why EQ is so important.
Living with people who have a different opinion from me is stressful .
We also need to live in harmony with each other. However, if you lack EQ,
you can face various problems because you cannot live with other people.
For example, it may be difficult to make friends, or you may be stressed when it comes to work because you and other people think differently.


As I worked, I understood the importance of EQ. It was empathy. When I was in my early 20s, in order to achieve my goals, only my opinions were important.
But as positions change in the workplace, achieving goals is also important.
But I've come to realize that empathizing and managing emotions among employees produces better results.
