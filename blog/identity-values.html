About my identity and values

With this experience, I tried to wait and understand even if beginner employees made a mistake.
We made the highest income one of branches.

The employees who worked with me have been promoted to managers of another new store

I believe that life cannot be lived alone.
It takes someone's patience and someone's consideration, but I think we can all work together and live well.
